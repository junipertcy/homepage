The Society of Young Network Scientists

What is the SYNS?

The SYNS is an organization designed to support early-career network scientists as they pursue research, to promote their work, and to shape the evolving field of network science.

How do I get involved?

Stop by one of the SYNS events! Alternatively, feel free to contact the organizers (me) with questions, or to get added to our Slack.

SYNS in Colorado!

With Allie Morgan and Hunter Wapman, we launched our inaugural local meeting in October 2019. In our 2nd meeting (Feb. 2020), we were lucky to have five speakers, each from a distinct expertise (Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science, Applied Math, Environmental Science, ...), to give a 5-min short research talk. Attendees are from Boulder, Fort Collins, and Denver area. If you are in our neck of the woods, poke us and be added to our Google Group and email list!

台灣網絡科學教育 (Network Science Education in Taiwan)

這是一個公益專案，該網站收集和台灣有關的網絡數據、案例研究，也收集網絡的建模與分析的課程資料。希望讓網絡科學更親近實際生活。
