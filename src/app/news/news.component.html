Hi there! I am a postdoctoral scholar at the BioFrontiers Institute at CU Boulder. Supported by The Kavli Foundation, I am developing an analytical approach to infer functional connectivity from neurophysiological data and investigate the evolution of network structure across behaviors. Overall, my research focuses on understanding complex systems in social and biological domains, by devising and using tools from statistical inference, computational topology and convex optimization.
