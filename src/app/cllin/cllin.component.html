Remembering Ching-Liang Lin (1931–2019)

Ching-Liang lectured my General Physics in 2007-08 at the National Taiwan University. She was incredibly generous with her time and was definitely a night owl. I liked to drop by her office for a chat, seeing lights from down the building. As a friend and a teacher, she influenced my views on physics and science education.

More of her views on education will be posted here later. (These notes are in Taiwan, but it's hard to travel during the pandemic.)

See her page at Wikipedia and 維基百科.
