Remembering Ching-Liang Lin (1931–2019)

Ching-Liang lectured my General Physics in 2007 at the National Taiwan University, when I was a freshman in college. She was incredibly generous with her time and attention, and was definitely a night owl. Occasionally when I saw lights from her office down the building, I liked to drop by and chat with her. As a friend and teacher, she influenced my views on physics and science education.

More of her views on education will be posted here later. (These notes are in Taiwan, but it's hard to travel during the pandemic.)

See her page at Wikipedia and 維基百科.
