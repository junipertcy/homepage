Remembering Ching-Liang Lin (1931–2019)

Ching-Liang lectured my General Physics in 2007-08 at the National Taiwan University (NTU). She was incredibly generous with her time and was definitely a night owl. At times when seeing lights from down her office, I liked to visit her for a chat. As a friend and a teacher, she influenced my views on physics and on science education.

Ching-Liang's memorial ceremony was held on Apr 30, 2022 in the Dept. of Physics, NTU.
